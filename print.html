Print Work

VEGAS INC redesign

I redesigned the business newspaper into an award winning magazine that reflected the life of Vegas. Within a 10-week deadline, the magazine went from concept to printing its first issue. As part of the redesign, I created an extensive style guide and established production workflows for the team to follow.

Cincinnati Enquirer redesign

I redesigned the Cincinnati Enquirer from a broadsheet to a compact, which allowed for new, dynamic storytelling in print. I often met with the design, production and newsroom leadership to make sure our process maximized storytelling in the new format.
